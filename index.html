About Me

I am Dr. Mohammad Nadeem, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Aligarh Muslim University. I specialize in Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Natural Language Processing. With a Ph.D. in Computer Science, my research focuses on developing innovative solutions in text mining, sentiment analysis, and computational intelligence. I am passionate about advancing the field through research and mentoring the next generation of computer scientists.

Research Interests

Machine Learning & Deep Learning
