About the Symposium

The 16th International Mineral Processing Symposium (IMPS 2018) was held at ODTÜ METU in Belek-Antalya, Turkey. The symposium brought together industry professionals and academics to discuss innovations and promote collaborations within the mineral processing industry.

The event featured presentations on cutting-edge research, technological advancements, and sustainable practices in mineral processing, providing a platform for knowledge exchange and networking among international experts in the field.
